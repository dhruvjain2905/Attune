Attune

An AI-powered system for gently aligning attention with intention. Understand how your attention moves during focused work—without rigid tracking or judgment.

Features

What Attune Does

Attune observes your screen activity and uses AI to detect moments of misalignment between intention and action.

visibility

Silent Observation

Periodically captures screenshots and analyzes them to understand your current activity. If you're on-task, Attune stays silent.

notifications

Gentle Nudges

When clear distraction is detected, receive a subtle notification—not to enforce discipline, but to restore awareness.

insights

Reflective Analytics

After each session, view insights on when attention drifted, types of distractions, and how focus changed over time.

Process

How It Works

1

Define Your Goal

Start a session and state your intended task or focus area.

2

Work Naturally

Attune runs quietly in the background while you work as usual.

3

Receive Nudges

Get subtle notifications only when attention clearly diverges from your goal.

4

Review Insights

End the session to explore your focus patterns and build better habits.
